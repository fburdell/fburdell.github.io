About

Hello, my name is Frank. I grew up in northern Virginia and currently reside in central Pennsylvania. I competed springboard in college and fell in love with print making at some point. My early interest in philosophy lead me down the rabbit hole of maths-- particularly, statistics.

Civic systems

I work as a systems and testing consultant with private and federal legislative clients to optimize technical stacks against human-centric specifications at the Kastling Group. I've been lucky to work with the Office of the Clerk and of the House Legislative Counsel within the U.S. House of Representatives, Democracy Fund in coordination with GovTrack, and good2go.

Political analysis

Previously, I worked as an operations and analytics director at a state caucus, and manager for some state-office races. I got to lead mid-scale campaign teams and learn about map making, targeting, and prediction during my time at the caucus.
