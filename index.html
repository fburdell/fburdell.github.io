About

Hello, my name is Frank. I grew up in northern Virginia and currently reside in central Pennsylvania. I competed springboard in college and fell in love with print making at some point. My early interest in philosophy lead me down the rabbit hole of maths-- particularly, statistics.

Civic systems

I work as a systems analyst with private and public federal legislative clients redesigning technical stacks to match human requirements. I've been lucky to work on such projects as the Comparative Print Project with the Clerk of the U.S. House, and BillMap in coordination with GovTrack and Demand Progress.

Political analysis

Previously, I worked as a analytics and operations director at a state caucus, and manager for some state-office races.
